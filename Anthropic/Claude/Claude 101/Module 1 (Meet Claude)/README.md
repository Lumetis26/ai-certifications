# Meet Claude

## Summary

Claude is an AI assistant designed to function as a collaborative thinking partner rather than a traditional chatbot. It excels at reasoning, writing, coding, research, analysis, and problem-solving by combining user expertise with AI capabilities. Effective collaboration with Claude relies on providing context, defining clear objectives, and iterating on responses instead of expecting perfect results from a single prompt.

---

### Effective Prompting

Successful prompts generally contain three elements:

1. **Set the Stage**
   - Explain your role.
   - Provide relevant context.
   - State your objective.

2. **Define the Task**
   - Clearly describe what Claude should do.

3. **Specify Rules**
   - Explain formatting, tone, style, constraints, or provide examples.

Example structure:

Context → Task → Rules

---

### Context Improves Results

Claude performs better when provided with additional context.

Useful context includes:

- Documents
- PDFs
- Images
- Spreadsheets
- Source code
- Background information

Providing relevant information reduces ambiguity and improves response quality.

---

### Iterative Collaboration

Working with Claude should be viewed as an ongoing conversation.

Effective users:

- Refine prompts
- Provide feedback
- Ask follow-up questions
- Restart conversations when necessary

The first response is typically the starting point rather than the finished product.

## Common Challenges

| Challenge | Improvement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Responses are too generic | Add more context and constraints. |
| Responses are too long or short | Specify the desired length. |
| Wrong format | Provide an example or define the structure explicitly. |
| Incorrect facts | Verify important information and request citations. |
| Tone isn't correct | Describe the desired tone or provide examples. |

## AI Fluency

Anthropic introduces the **4D Framework** for effective AI collaboration.

### Delegation

Determine which work should be completed by AI versus humans.

### Description

Clearly communicate objectives, constraints, and desired outputs.

### Discernment

Critically evaluate AI responses for quality and accuracy.

### Diligence

Use AI responsibly while maintaining accountability for final work.

---

## Evaluating Claude

Rather than assuming Claude performs equally well on every task, evaluate it against your own workflows.

Simple evaluation process:

1. Collect examples of existing work.
2. Create prompts that replicate those tasks.
3. Compare Claude's output to your original work.
4. Refine prompts based on observed strengths and weaknesses.

This helps determine where Claude provides the most value.
